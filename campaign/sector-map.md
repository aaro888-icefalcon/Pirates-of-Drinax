## The four powers that frame the board
- **Third Imperium** *(coreward/trailing)* — the lazy giant. 3 subsectors (Pax Rulin = military bulwark, Gazulin = culture, **Tobia = ducal seat + sector fleet**). Won't expand except by client states; **doesn't recognise Drinax's marque** (privateer = pirate). Bleed a megacorp or an escorted convoy and the **Navy** sweeps the Borderlands for months. The teeth you must not wake.
- **Aslan Hierate** *(rimward/spinward; holds the rimward half)* — **no central authority**, dozens of feuding clans, each its own navy; driven by trade corporations and the **ihatei** land-hunger. Growing 10–20% this century. Key clans: **Ahroay'if** (Tlaiowaha, trade-rich, pro-peace — *the clan that bombed Drinax in 885*), **Hrakoea** (Borderland, aggressive), **Tiykhisto** (pro-war). Raid Aslan shipping → relentless **honour-vendetta**; raid *Imperial* shipping → the Aslan quietly applaud.
- **Florian League** *(spinward)* — Ancient-engineered Floriani (Feskals + Barnai), TL15, ultra-conservative, lottery government, isolationist. Transactional/indifferent to Drinax; **overwhelming nuclear force** only if threatened.
- **Glorious Empire** *(rimward, Goertal/Nora'a)* — breakaway **Aslan slaver** state; xenophobic, always at war, declining; the sector bogeyman, no real allies.

## The wild middle — your playground (lawless "Outrim Void")
- **Tlaiowaha** — HOME. Drinax + the scattered independent worlds of the old Kingdom (Asim, Clarke, Torpol, Pourne, Paal, Blue, Hilfer…). Aslan-held on paper (Ahroay'if), full of courtable independents. The **ihatei muster at Kteiroa** is here — the existential clock.
- **Sindal** *(coreward of Tlaiowaha)* — **Theev** (your Friendly pirate haven/fence/yard), **Noricum** (the dead Sindalian capital — viral ruins + the rumoured buried treasury / your crown-jewels lead), the Sindalian Main, Oghman raider clans, an NE-corner Imperial naval post (Realgar).
- **The Borderland** *(coreward; the trade corridor)* — where the dust-spice route runs; **GeDeCo's** turf; Hrakoea Aslan pressing in. Prime pirate-ambush waters at **Argona & Sperle**.
- **Dpres** (the Dustbelt — independent worlds at constant war), **Egyrn** (Outrim Void; **Belgardian Sojournate** pirate base), **Menorial** (Strend Cluster — anti-Imperial Solomani, TL15), **Binary** (Great Rift edge, jump-6).

## The major non-state players
- **GeDeCo** (General Development Company) — the Reach's biggest non-state actor; builds starports, courts development worlds, runs spies; **HQ at Vorito (0709, near you)**; believes Drinax's fall is inevitable. **Your direct rival empire-builder** for the same worlds.
- **The Pirate Lords of Theev** — 50–100 raider bands. **Admiral Darokyn** (your conditional "friend" — and the Imperium's secret agent), **Petyr Vallis** (Aslan-hating zealot), **Hroal Irontooth** (Aslan loot-pirate). Your hub for fence/repair/recruit/intel.
- **The Zhodani** — no bases, but 2–5 cruisers patrol the spinward Reach yearly and court independents.
- **The ihatei tide** — landless Aslan second-sons mustering (500+ at Kteiroa) to conquer a world. The standing threat to everything you build.

## The lifeline (and the hunting ground)
- **Hierate route** (busy): Fist → Wildeman → Cordan → Tech-World → Paal → **Tyokh** (~7 J-2 jumps / ~55 days). Imperial escorts Fist→Acrid; Ahroay'if escorts from Tech-World. **Argona & Sperle = ambush gold.**
- **Florian route** (long, ~140 days): Fist → Arunisiir → through Tlaiowaha (Blue, Torpol, Oghma) → the Sindalian Main → Yggdrasil. Largely unescorted past Arunisiir.
- **The whole point of the privateer scheme:** make these routes so unsafe the great powers find it cheaper to *recognise a reborn Drinax* as the route's protector than to crush you.

## Where you sit
Tlaiowaha — the rimward-trailing wild center: Imperium coreward/trailing, Aslan rimward/spinward, the pirate Sindal at your back, the trade corridor (Borderland) at your shoulder, GeDeCo competing for the same prizes, and the ihatei storm gathering rimward. A knife-edge frontier — exactly the kind of place a kingdom gets carved out of in blood and steel.
