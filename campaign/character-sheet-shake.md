> The player character. Captain of the Harrier; physician by training. Built via the Traveller 2e lifepath (characteristics adapted from the player's own profile by agreement; career history rolled honestly).

## Identity
- **Name:** Abhishek "Shake" Rao — goes by **Shake**.
- **Species / origin:** Human, born in the **Third Imperium** (core, not the Reach). An **outsider** in the Trojan Reach — no title, no court connections. That outsider status is exactly why Oleb's marque is his one shot at *being someone* out here.
- **Age:** 30 · **Terms:** 2 (Scholar 1, Navy 1) + University (graduated **with honours**).
- **Rank/credentials:** Attending physician (Scholar rank 1); commissioned **Navy Ensign** (ret.) — a ship's medical officer who took a commission and learned a bridge.
- **Look & manner:** slim, 5'11"; calm, dry, emotionally contained; practical, focused on the next step. Blunt when task-focused. Gives people autonomy — sometimes too much. Improvises well under pressure; loop-closing and bookkeeping can slip. Lapsed Tae Kwon Do.
