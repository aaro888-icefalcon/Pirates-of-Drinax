# Seed Sources — Trojan Reach   (hook: seeds)
- deck size: 35            # 30–40
- refresh: each bookkeeping
- sources:
  - **setting-canon entries near the PC** — the worlds, factions, and NPCs in the crew's current subsector/route
    (`setting-canon.md`; drill into `../references/setting/worlds/00_world-index.md`, `../references/setting/personalities-and-factions.md`).
  - **live world state** — the active doom clocks (Imperial Heat, ihatei pressure, Drinax decay, rival pirates),
    revealed regions, per-world Attitudes, court standing, and open Threads (`campaign/campaign-state.md`, `campaign/reputation-tracker.md`).
  - **the open adventures** — clusters/fragments from any live episode and the campaign spine (`adventures/pirates-of-drinax.md`).
  - **random rolls for novelty** — `generators/rumour_of_the_reach.json` (patron/adventure hooks),
    `generators/prey_ship.json` (when raiding), `generators/reach_hazard.json` (travel/sandbox),
    `generators/npc_role.json` (a new face).
- consumed when a seed is invoked by a scene / Turning Point / Random Event; replace consumed seeds at the next refresh.
