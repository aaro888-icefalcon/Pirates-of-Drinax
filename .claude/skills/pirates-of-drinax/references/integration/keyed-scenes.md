# Pirates of Drinax — Keyed Scenes (mythic-gm Prepared-Adventure layer)

> Use this when: running the Reach sandbox. **Copy into the campaign folder** as `keyed-scenes.md`. Check these triggers during bookkeeping each scene. Per-adventure "must-happen" beats live inside each `adventures/NN_*.md`; this is the campaign-level trigger layer. Seed the Lists from `../campaign/seed-lists.md`.

**Mode = Prepared Adventure (sandbox):** the Reach is *available content the oracle plays through honestly*, never a fixed order. The dice decide; adapt when they overturn the script. Honour **Player ≠ PC knowledge** — an adventure becomes "live" only when the crew reach its trigger, and its secrets stay gated until earned.

## Campaign spine (the through-line)
| Trigger | Event |
|---|---|
| Session start; crew hear the King's summons | Run **01 Honour Among Thieves** — Oleb's offer & test. *(must happen first)* |
| Honour Among Thieves resolved | The sandbox opens: marque + Harrier in hand, the Reach is theirs. Seed sandbox Threads/Features. |
| Imperial **Heat** clock fills (several big raids / a notorious atrocity) | **06 The Game of Sun & Shadow** goes live — the Imperium hunts the privateers. Check monthly: `dice.py keyed 1d10 <target>`. |
| Drinax's recruited Fighting Strength crosses a milestone, or ~2/3 through play | Succession & coup pressures rise → **09 Blood of the Star Dragon**. |
| Kingdom large enough to alarm the powers, or the player declares the endgame | **10 Finale** — great-power response; outcome set by the empire/reputation state. |

## Travel & sandbox (check per jump / per visit)
| Trigger | Event |
|---|---|
| Each jump through patrolled/Amber space | Patrol or Navy encounter check (`../rules/piracy-raiding.md` detection; else a Fate Question). |
| Crew choose a hunting ground & go raiding | Run the **commerce-raiding procedure** (`../rules/piracy-raiding.md`). |
| Arrive at any world | Its **Attitude** colours reception (`../campaign/empire-reputation.md`); a patron hook from **11 Patrons & Opportunities** may surface (Fate Question / Random Event). |
| Arrive at Theev or another haven | Fencing & black market open (`../rules/piracy-raiding.md`). |
| Fate-Question doubles ≤ CF with focus on an Adventure Feature | Pull a Reach hazard/opportunity from the Features List. |
| Refuel at a gas giant / wilderness | Skimming + processing risk (`../rules/starship-operations.md`). |

## Bookkeeping additions (run with mythic-gm's normal bookkeeping)
Advance the **sandbox clocks** — Imperial Heat, Aslan ihatei pressure, Drinax's decay, rival pirates. Update per-world **Attitudes** and the Drinax **Fighting Strength**. Update the **Harrier**'s condition & finances. Then Chaos/Lists/state as usual.
