# Personalities & Recurring NPCs of the Trojan Reach

> Use this when: you must voice a named NPC the crew meets across the campaign — court, rival ruler, Aslan noble, Imperial agent, Theev pirate, or patron. **Play each one to win:** every NPC has a WANT and acts on it whether or not the PCs help. For the powers behind them see `factions.md`; for worlds see `worlds/00_world-index.md`; for ship stats see `ships-of-the-reach.md`.

## How to use
1. Each entry: **role/affiliation · location · stat line · personality-in-a-phrase · WANTS · attitude to Drinax/crew · appears in.** Run the NPC toward its WANT.
2. **Stat-line provenance.** Lines marked **(stats: PoD1)** are reproduced from a printed stat block. Lines marked **(reconstruct/verify)** had only narrative in the converted sources — the full block lives on a PDF page (`Pirates of Drinax Appendixes`, the *Index of Cast & Characters*) that did not convert; the values shown are reasonable reconstructions, **verify against the PDF before a fight matters.** Never invent precision you don't have.
3. **GM-Only** lines are secrets/twists — reveal only as earned. Withhold them when narrating.
4. Aslan: run every entry through the honour rules + reaction table in `factions.md`.

## THE COURT OF DRINAX
*Cross-ref `drinax.md` (the Floating Palace) and `factions.md` (Kingdom of Drinax). The crew return here again and again; these are their default Allies/Contacts/Patrons.* (PoD2: "The Kingdom of Drinax / Non-Player Characters")

### King Oleb XVI (ruler)
- **Role/affiliation:** King of Drinax, self-styled "Dragon Emperor of Sindal-in-Exile, Lord Admiral of the Star Guard." The crew's **patron** — issues the Harrier and the letter of marque.
- **Location:** The Floating Palace, **Drinax** (Tlaiowaha/Drinax subsector).
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** SOC 14 INT 10 EDU 9 · skills *Leadership 3, Diplomat 2 (when he bothers), Melee (blade) 2, Flyer (grav) 2, Tactics (military) 1, Carouse 3, Persuade 2, Gambler 1* · gear: ceremonial **grav belt** & rapier, hawk-men honour guard. Age 105 (anagathics; apparent mid-50s, hugely overweight).
- **Personality:** a bellowing, gluttonous buffoon who is secretly a cunning statesman.
- **WANTS:** to **rebuild the Kingdom of Drinax** by making piracy pay for ships, allies and respectability — and to enjoy drink, women, food and flying while he still can. Privately regrets conquering Asim.
- **Attitude to crew:** their **maker and master** — generous, demanding, easily slighted, never forgets a debt; expects results and his cut.
- **Appears in:** the whole campaign (frame & patron; "The Pirates of Drinax," PoD2).

### Princess Rao (heir & chief counsellor)
- **Role:** Crown Princess of Drinax; Oleb's foremost diplomat and adviser; named heir while Harrick lay dying.
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify; full block Appendixes p241):** SOC 12 INT 11 EDU 11 · skills *Diplomat 3, Admin 2, Persuade 2, Advocate 1, Leadership 1, Carouse 1* · gear: noble's wardrobe, comm, light sidearm. Age 28.
- **Personality:** brilliant, idealistic, quietly steers the kingdom past her father's bluster.
- **WANTS:** a **grand alliance of worlds** — Drinax reborn, *or* a more equal progressive league against the great powers — and to bring TL15 knowledge back to the old subject worlds for a golden age.
- **Attitude to crew:** their best ally at court; favours **subtle empire-building over crude piracy**; will quietly back, fund and cover for a crew that builds real alliances.
- **GM-Only:** detractors are right that her grand alliance, pursued clumsily, will **provoke the Aslan**; she may overreach.
- **Appears in:** throughout; central to endgame "Grave of Empires."

### Prince Harrick (rival heir)
- **Role:** Prince of Drinax, the *other* potential heir; led the Asim invasion 20 years ago.
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify; full block Appendixes p240):** STR 9 DEX 8 END 10 INT 7 EDU 8 SOC 12 · skills *Tactics (military) 2, Gun Combat (energy) 1, Leadership 1, Pilot (small craft) 1* · gear: cybernetics throughout. Age 54.
- **Personality:** stilted, too-perfect skin, "more machine and cloned tissue than man" — emerged from a regrowth tank only six months ago.
- **WANTS:** to **reclaim his place and his honour** after 20 years lost; military glory; the throne Rao now stands to inherit.
- **Attitude to crew:** wary; a potential **rival/Enemy** to a crew aligned with Rao, an Ally to one who champions the old martial Drinax.
- **GM-Only:** his resurrection is barely understood even by the Scholar's Tower; his loyalties and stability are a campaign wildcard (succession crisis hook).
- **Appears in:** court intrigue; succession plots.

### Lord Wrax (commander, Star Guard)
- **Role:** Commands the **Star Guard** (Drinax's navy — a handful of sub-400-ton ships; only the 100-ton scouts jump).
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** SOC 10 INT 9 EDU 8 · skills *Tactics (naval) 3, Leadership 2, Pilot (spacecraft) 2, Gunner 1, Admin 1* · gear: dress uniform, sidearm.
- **Personality:** proud professional officer who resents amateurish piracy.
- **WANTS:** the Harrier as **his** flagship and a real fleet; has **little time for Oleb's pirate scheme.**
- **Attitude to crew:** disdainful of "privateers"; refused Kasiyl a Star Guard berth; can become a grudging ally if the crew prove genuinely useful to Drinax's defence.
- **Appears in:** court; fleet matters; "Grave of Empires."

### Scholar Voha (court sage)
- **Role:** Elderly master of the **Scholar's Tower**, university chancellor, unofficial court prognosticator and keeper of Sindalian knowledge.
- **Location:** Scholar's Tower, Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 12 EDU 13 SOC 9 · skills *Science (multiple) 3, Astrogation 2, History/Education 3, Advocate 1, Admin 1* · frail, aged.
- **Personality:** cautious custodian of secrets, jealous of the Palace's safety.
- **WANTS:** to **preserve the ancient knowledge of Sindal** and keep the Floating Palace intact; wary of any bold action that endangers it.
- **Attitude to crew:** a source of **Sindalian lore, codes and tech leads** (drives "Shadows of Sindal" hooks) — but obstructs schemes he deems reckless.
- **Appears in:** Sindalian-relic adventures; tech-upgrade questlines.

### Sal Dancet (court free trader & fixer)
- **Role:** Free trader and smuggler who uses the Palace for repairs and a bolt-hole.
- **Location:** mobile; based out of the Floating Palace.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 9 EDU 8 SOC 7 · skills *Broker 3, Streetwise 2, Deception 1, Persuade 2, Pilot (spacecraft) 2* · light sidearm, fast trader.
- **Personality:** breezy, well-connected, knows where everything is.
- **WANTS:** profit and good standing at court; rare goods to move.
- **Attitude to crew:** ready **Contact for rare commodities, rumours and information**; a buy/sell fence for the crew.
- **Appears in:** anytime the crew need goods/intel via court.

### Cleon Hardy (ombudsman of Asim)
- **Role:** Elected representative of conquered **Asim** at Oleb's court; ex-economist & broadcaster.
- **Location:** Floating Palace (from Asim).
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 9 EDU 9 SOC 6 · skills *Admin 2, Advocate 2, Broker 1, Diplomat 1* · civilian.
- **Personality:** earnest, out of his depth in the bizarre court, learning fast.
- **WANTS:** fair treatment and a real voice for **Asim's five million peasants**; to keep the tithe bearable.
- **Attitude to crew:** sympathetic if they treat Asim decently; a lever on the **Asim subplot** (vassal-world unrest).
- **Appears in:** Asim-related events; reputation effects of how the crew treat subject worlds.

### Chieftain Galx (Vespexer tribal envoy)
- **Role:** Envoy of the **Vespexer** surface tribes of Drinax, visiting when the Palace passes overhead.
- **Location:** Drinax surface / Floating Palace.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** STR 9 END 10 INT 7 SOC 6 · skills *Survival 3, Recon 2, Melee 1, Streetwise 1* · always in a **full hazard suit** (won't doff it even indoors).
- **Personality:** hardy, proud surface-dweller distrustful of palace softness.
- **WANTS:** respect, trade goods and protection for the surface clans.
- **Attitude to crew:** a possible starting **Contact** (chargen); ally if the crew honour the tribes.
- **Appears in:** Drinax-surface scenes.

### Lady Hil (snobbish courtier)
- **Role:** Influential noble obsessed with lineage.
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** SOC 12 INT 8 EDU 9 · skills *Diplomat 1, Carouse 2, Deception 1, Persuade 1.*
- **Personality:** haughty; "has little time for anyone who cannot trace their lineage."
- **WANTS:** prestige and the old order; to look down on upstarts.
- **Attitude to crew:** disdains low-born privateers; a minor court **obstacle/Rival** who can be flattered or out-maneuvered.
- **Appears in:** court intrigue.

*(Kasiyl of the Ahroay'if also resides at court — listed under the Aslan below.)*

## RULERS & WARLORDS OF KEY WORLDS
*The independent rulers Drinax must court, cower or topple. Full UWPs in `worlds/00_world-index.md`.*

### The Foundation of Asim (deposed) → Drinaxi nobility (incumbent)
- **Role/affiliation:** Asim's old ruling council (the **Foundation**, a 1,000-year bureaucratic scam), overthrown 20 yrs ago; now ruled by **Drinaxi nobles** Oleb installed.
- **Location:** **Asim** (Tlaiowaha) — non-industrial TL6, ~5 million.
- **Personality (faction):** the Foundation = self-serving sages; the new nobles = absentee overlords.
- **WANTS:** the Foundation's remnants want their power back; the nobles want a quiet, productive tithe-world.
- **Attitude to Drinax:** Asim is **Drinax's only vassal** — resentful peasantry, fragile loyalty. A flashpoint if the crew mishandle it.
- **Appears in:** the Asim subplot; reputation arc.

### Lady Yemar (ruler of Palindrome)
- **Role:** Self-styled "Lady," **former pirate** turned sovereign of Astrogo; rules ~5,000 followers, plus a ruling elite of ex-pirate associates who form Palindrome's police, military and money.
- **Location:** **Astrogo**, domed town on **Palindrome** (Sindal subsector); a TL12 enclave near the **Theev Cluster**.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 9 EDU 8 SOC 9 · skills *Leadership 2, Gun Combat 1, Tactics 1, Broker 2, Streetwise 2* · **chronically ill** — a bioweapon during a raid left her with severe medical needs only treatable on **Theev** (her leash).
- **Personality:** a retired raider who bought respectability and guards it ruthlessly.
- **WANTS:** comfort, security and quiet; to **launder her pirate fortune** (buys/sells cargo *shares*, not the ramshackle port) and shelter fellow fugitives — while keeping Imperial agents out of Astrogo.
- **Attitude to Drinax/crew:** transactional; her town clears **pirate cargoes** for those with the right contacts and is a discreet market. Attacking ships near Palindrome is suicide — raiders who try "turn up destroyed as a warning."
- **GM-Only:** her dependence on Theev's medics makes her **suborn-able** — fix her medical problem and you own a world.
- **Appears in:** Theev-cluster operations; fencing loot; Palindrome (PoD2/Companion).

## THE ASLAN HIERATE
*Run with the honour ladder, duelling rules and reaction table in `factions.md`. Males flaunt clan & rank; females run money and tech.*

### Kasiyl of the Ahroay'if (exiled prince, at Drinax court)
- **Role:** Exiled **Aslan noble** of the **Ahroay'if** (dominant clan of Tlaiowaha subsector); now bodyguard & interpreter at Drinax.
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify; full block Appendixes p197):** STR 12 DEX 11 END 10 INT 8 SOC (low — landless) · skills *Melee (claw/natural) 3, Pilot (spacecraft) 3, Gun Combat 2, Recon 1, Tactics 1* · **dewclaws**; no facility with tech or money (typical high-class male).
- **Personality:** a mighty warrior and superlative pilot, proud but penniless and adrift among humans.
- **WANTS:** honour, **territory and a place** — he sought (and was refused) a Star Guard berth by Lord Wrax. Refuses to speak of why he fled the Hierate.
- **Attitude to crew:** a potential loyal **Aslan ally/Contact** (chargen pick) and a window into the Hierate; bound by Aslan honour.
- **GM-Only:** *why* a prince of the dominant clan fled is an unrevealed mystery — a hook the GM defines.
- **Appears in:** court; Aslan-facing diplomacy.

### Asaeai'oul (mysterious Aslan power-broker)
- **Role/affiliation:** Aslan leader of **deliberately obscured clan & rank**; small entourage, lives aboard a private yacht. *Asset (Faction Size 0).*
- **Location:** itinerant — backwater ports to major installations, ~2 weeks each, no pattern.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 11 EDU 11 SOC 10 · skills *Diplomat 2, Admin 2, Broker 2, Deception 1, Tactics 1, Tolerance 2* · entourage of mercenaries/couriers; honourable, never lies.
- **Personality:** condescending courtesy to all; deals honourably but "only gives information he wants others to have."
- **WANTS:** to **prepare an *ihatei* surge into the Reach he expects to fail** — so that the leading rival clans exhaust themselves and his own clan scoops up their territory afterward.
- **Attitude to crew:** may **deal with Drinax short-term** to maximise his rivals' casualties; useful for info/equipment; "the upstart barbarian kingdom can be dealt with later."
- **GM-Only:** another high-status Aslan *would* recognise his honour-titles — he is open about his clan to peers, opaque to "barbarians." Clever crews can use him as a **conduit to feed false intel into the Hierate.**
- **Appears in:** Aslan intrigue; *ihatei* arc (Companion: Personalities & Factions).

### Notable *ihatei* leaders — the "Ihatei!" warlords
*The four nobles most likely to attract a "critical mass" of followers in the border camp. Whoever the crew elevate or remove decides where the swarm falls. 25-week clock — see `adventures/`. (PoD1: "Ihatei!", Notable Ihatei)*

**Eihei the Clever (Tiykhisto clan)** — Aslan male, age 24. **(stats: PoD1)** STR 9 DEX 7 END 8 INT 11 EDU 10 SOC 9 · skills *Astrogation 1, Electronics (sensors) 1, Engineer 0, Gun Combat (slug) 3, Independence 2, Leadership 2, Melee (unarmed) 1, Persuade 1, Pilot (spacecraft) 1, Recon 2, Science 0, Survival 1, Tolerance 1, Vacc Suit 0* · **Combat Armour (15)** w/ grav assist & computer weave (computer/1), **Claw Edging (3D+1)**, **Gauss Pistol (3D, AP 3, Auto 2)**.
- Personality: cultured, well-travelled, fights *scientifically*. WANTS: to **conquer Drinax as a high-tech staging post** (identified with broker Fyukh's help) for invading Tlaiowaha/Borderland. Attitude: a coldly rational threat — one of two early advocates for invading Drinax.

**Iykhi Too-Eager (Htyowao clan)** — Aslan male, age 28. **(stats: PoD1)** STR 13(19) DEX 5 END 8 INT 7 EDU 8 SOC 7 · skills *Gun Combat (slug) 3, Heavy Weapons (man portable) 2, Leadership 2, Melee (natural) 1, Persuade 1, Recon 2, Stealth 2, Tactics (military) 2, Tolerance 0, Vacc Suit 2* · **Aslan Battle Dress (19, STR+6, DEX+3)**, **Taoyuhrir Heavy MG (4D+3, Auto 3)**; 800-ton *Ekawsiykua*-class escort overhead.
- Personality: charismatic but death-wishing; his clan is losing the Keaih war. WANTS: secretly **a glorious death** rather than watch the Htyowao decline; commands real followers. Attitude: dangerous because he doesn't care if he lives.

**Toiho the Brave (Hkaaiheir clan)** — Aslan male, age 24. **(stats: PoD1)** STR 9(15) DEX 8 END 10 INT 8 SOC 12 · skills *Athletics (dexterity) 2, Carouse 1, Diplomat 2, Gun Combat (energy) 2, Heavy Weapons (man portable) 2, Leadership 2, Melee (unarmed) 2, Persuade 2, Recon 1, Survival 1, Tactics (military) 2, Vacc Suit 2* · **Aslan Battle Dress (19, STR+6, DEX+3)**, **Gravity Spear (2D, AP Special)**; two holovid crews follow him.
- Personality: brash, invincible-feeling media darling. WANTS: **glory and legend outside the Hierate** — eyeing waterworld **Pourne** (needs a whole clan) but undecided where to "plant his claw." Attitude: condescending; the most romantic rallying figure.

**Old Elyo (no clan — Outcast)** — Aslan male, age 52. **(stats: PoD1)** STR 10 DEX 6 END 8 INT 7 EDU 8 SOC 8 · skills *Gun Combat (slug) 4, Leadership 1, Melee (unarmed) 3, Persuade 0, Pilot (small craft) 1, Recon 3, Stealth 2, Tactics (military) 2, Vacc Suit 2* · **Combat Armour (15)**, **Autorifle (3D, Auto 2)**.
- Personality: the camp's most skilled and scarred warrior, but landless, "unlucky," and bitter. WANTS: **Territory at last** — every night he demands an attack on the rich human world **Paal** (heavily defended via Vorito/GeDeCo arms). Attitude: distrustful of clan manipulation; tired of being used as a weapon.

## THE IMPERIUM (agents, officers, consuls)
*The lazy giant's local faces. It ignores small fish — until a consul or agent fingers the Harrier. (factions.md: Third Imperium)*

### Imperial Consul Thao Poloc (consul at Drinax)
- **Role:** Third Imperium's **consul** at Oleb's court; a historian by vocation.
- **Location:** Floating Palace, Drinax.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 10 EDU 12 SOC 10 · skills *Admin 2, Advocate 1, History/Education 3, Diplomat 2, Investigate 1* · civilian; comm to (distant) Imperial channels.
- **Personality:** a mild academic who came to study the last remnants of Sindal.
- **WANTS:** to **research Sindal** in peace; nominally handle lost merchant ships and do "a little light spying."
- **Attitude to Drinax/crew:** low-key; a reminder Capital is watching. **Leak risk** — what he reports home can put the crew on an Imperial list; cultivate or feed him.
- **GM-Only:** "a little light spying" means his despatches are an intelligence channel; befriending or misleading him shapes Imperial heat.
- **Appears in:** court; whenever Imperial attention is in play.

### Admiral Darokyn (renegade Imperial officer; pirate admiral of Theev)
- **Role/affiliation:** Self-styled "Admiral"; **ex-Imperial Navy officer** who defected ~30 yrs ago with a flotilla; commands the largest organised force out of **Theev**.
- **Location:** **Theev** (Sindal subsector); meets contacts at his table in the **Rose Pavilion**, Upper City. Flagship **Sorrowful Maiden**.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** STR 7 DEX 8 END 9 INT 11 EDU 11 SOC 12 · skills *Tactics (naval) 3, Leadership 3, Pilot (spacecraft) 2, Gunner 2, Admin 2, Diplomat 2, Gun Combat 1* · crisp **naval uniform** (only insignia: a silver skull-and-crossbones + golden "D"); anagathics, late 60s.
- **Fleet:** a flotilla of **~30 ships** (sub-1,000-ton interceptors/support craft he stole from the Navy); the salvager **Mercifuge** is on the books as one of his (now Ferrik's). Most disciplined pirate force in the Reach.
- **Personality:** cultured, urbane, ramrod-straight — "acts like a model naval officer at the Emperor's dinner," not a thief in a den.
- **WANTS:** to **run the most professional operation on Theev** with minimal bloodshed and maximum control; and (above all) to keep his **secret** buried.
- **Attitude to crew:** courteous and businesslike; deals only with those who pass a **Difficult (10+) Diplomat (SOC) check or SOC 10+.** Hungry for capable new blood.
- **GM-Only — Darokyn's Secret:** he is **still a deep-cover agent of Imperial Intelligence** — they secretly fund his gang and use him as their spy/agent in the wild Reach. **Ferrik Redthane discovered this** (and recorded intercepted comms from Imperial space, stored on a secure computer aboard the **Sorrowful Maiden**) — which is *why* Darokyn wants Ferrik dead and silenced. Proof of the secret lets the crew **blackmail Darokyn or destroy his whole organisation.** Never admit, even obliquely, why Ferrik fled. (PoD1: "Belgard"/Theev; *Darokyn's Secret*)
- **Appears in:** "Belgard" / the Theev chapter; recurs as patron-or-target thereafter.

## THEEV & THE PIRATE LORDS
*Theev (Blacksand) is the Reach's pirate world — chaos "presided over," not ruled, by the Pirate Lords. Identities are guarded; assassination between lords is routine. (PoD2/Companion: Theev; PoD1: "Belgard")*

### The Lords of Theev / Lords of Blacksand (the ruling cabal)
- **Role:** Cabal of warlords/crime-bosses who hold the Upper City & "The Skull," profit from the violence, and keep Theev in business by steering Imperial heat elsewhere. Some hide behind a "blind" front-man.
- **Location:** Blacksand, **Theev**.
- **Law of the Lords:** "no murder in the Upper City" — enforced with perfect lethality by the **Blacksand Widows** (below). Lower City = lawless.
- **WANTS:** profit, anonymity, and **Theev's continued survival** — they deliberately make *other* systems look like piracy hotspots to draw the Navy off, and lean on GeDeCo's quiet protection.
- **Attitude to crew:** tolerate useful pirates; lethal to anyone who threatens the equilibrium.
- **GM-Only:** several Lords' identities are unknown; **GeDeCo** secretly shields Theev for its own profit/leverage (see `factions.md`).

### Blacksand Widow (the Lords' enforcers)
- **Role:** Elite enforcer-assassins who police the Law of the Lords. Encountered as a type, not one person.
- **Stat line (stats: PoD1):** STR 10 DEX 14 END 12 INT 7 EDU 10 SOC 9 · skills *Athletics (dexterity) 2, Deception 1, Diplomat 0, Electronics (comms) 1, Flyer (grav) 2, Gun Combat (energy) 3, Gun Combat (slug) 3, Investigate 2, Medic 1, Melee (unarmed) 3, Persuade 1, Steward 1, Streetwise 2* · **Enhanced Vision, Neural Comm (TL14)**; **Lightweight Polycarapace (+12) + Subdermal Armour (+3)**; **Gauss Rifle (4D), Laser Pistol (3D+3), Stunner (3D)**.
- **Personality:** "never says a word; just smiles like light glinting off a razor." Even psychopaths won't cross the Widows.
- **WANTS:** to enforce the Lords' law flawlessly. Attitude to crew: neutral until a rule is broken, then lethal.

### Petyr Vallis (anti-Aslan raider)
- **Role/affiliation:** Rising **pirate gang leader** on Theev, recruiting hard. Hates the Aslan.
- **Location:** Theev — base in the lower stories of a **red-flagged** tower. Flagship-class force: ~**8 ships** incl. two *Fiery*-class 400-ton gunships and two 600-ton corsairs.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify; full block Appendixes p144):** STR 8 DEX 11 END 9 INT 9 SOC 7 · skills *Gun Combat 3, Melee (blade) 2, Tactics (military) 2, Leadership 2, Stealth 2, Recon 1, Pilot (spacecraft) 1* · pale-blue eyes, scar-latticed face; light armour, blades + sidearm.
- **Personality:** charismatic, cat-balanced, **borderline psychopath** (smiles at the wrong moments; "don't talk — roar; don't negotiate — demand").
- **WANTS:** **bloody vengeance on all Aslan** — slavers of the Glorious Empire killed his wife and child and maimed him; he especially wants Hroal Irontooth destroyed. Otherwise: lots of money.
- **Attitude to crew:** deals **on fair terms** if they'll join his war on the Aslan; otherwise demands cash. Wants to use the crew's **havens as launch platforms** against the Hierate. (Allying with him via "Belgard" drops crew standing with the Hierate by 1D.)
- **GM-Only:** his gang is unstable and will collapse in a few years once revenge palls — a knife aimed at Aslan colonies *now*.
- **Appears in:** "Belgard" (Theev) and recurs (Appendixes-referenced later adventures).

### Hroal Irontooth (Aslan outcast pirate)
- **Role/affiliation:** **Aslan outcast** who left the Hierate for power/glory/wealth; pure loot-pirate. Hated by Petyr Vallis.
- **Location:** Theev — holds court in a dive called the **Bilge Pump**. Targets Imperial shipping in Borderlands subsector.
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** STR 14(20) DEX 9 END 12 INT 7 SOC (low — outcast) · skills *Melee (natural/claw) 3, Gun Combat 2, Tactics (military) 2, Leadership 2, Recon 1, Vacc Suit 2* · **cybernetic jaws that bite through battle dress** (the "iron tooth"); battle dress.
- **Fleet:** ~**50** blood-thirsty mixed-species crew (human, Aslan, Vargr, two Yonts, an uplifted ape named **Blackfur**); flagship a **1,200-ton Halaheike-class pocket warship**, plus converted freighters/scouts.
- **Personality:** the most conventional of the three — "be all the pirate you can be"; proud, savage, never pushed around.
- **WANTS:** loot, power and (secretly) **the territory and home he misses** — he sublimates his Aslan land-hunger into violence and won't admit homesickness.
- **Attitude to crew:** will help if they **promise Oleb will grant the Aslan territory** in a restored Drinax — or pay him ≥Cr50,000; plots to seize the "Treasure Ship" navigation prize. (PoD1: "Belgard," "Treasure Ship")
- **Appears in:** "Belgard," "Treasure Ship" (his flotilla lurks at Exe), and beyond.

### Miria Silverhand (Ferrik's lieutenant; ex-victim turned raider)
- **Role:** Ferrik Redthane's lieutenant, emissary and fixer on Theev; ex-liner steward enslaved by pirates, now a hardened raider.

## PATRONS, FIXERS & CONTACTS (Drinaxian Companion)
*Asset/Faction-rated resources — mostly non-combatants who sell help and **spawn new tasks.** Interaction costs assume the Companion's Asset/Faction economy (see `factions.md`). (Companion, Ch.33 "Personalities and Factions")*

### Enigmatix (predictive computer "patron")
- **Type:** Individual / Asset (Size 0) — **not a person**, a TL-high analysis & prediction computer aboard a small ship, run by self-styled "owners."
- **What it does:** solves orbital-mechanics & ship-movement problems, reconstructs past positions, predicts likely systems/dates. A custom data pack ≈ a team working for weeks; **fee always >Cr100,000** per real question (no refunds on vague queries).
- **WANTS (its handlers):** quiet, controlled profit and the occasional favour; the machine itself "has no agenda — its function is to function."
- **Attitude to crew:** problem-solver-for-hire that **bills in favours and follow-on tasks** as much as cash.
- **GM-Only:** **not self-aware** (despite legends it "engineered its own thefts"); may be quietly **building a picture of the entire Reach for unknown purposes**, and could carry buried directives that surface later. Stealing it is a major op — it will **help its owners stop the theft** unless the crew feed it data convincing it it's safer elsewhere.

### Raif Zentoulli (high-end fixer)
- **Type:** Individual / Asset (Size 0). A loyalty-free **information & services fixer** who deals in leads, not goods.
- **Location:** mobile, often incognito aboard secure liners; appears even at enemies' parties (with bodyguards).
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 11 EDU 10 SOC 9 · skills *Streetwise 3, Broker 2, Deception 2, Investigate 2, Persuade 2, Admin 1* · bodyguards, remote scanners.
- **Personality:** a "spider" — hard-but-fair, secretive, deeply networked.
- **WANTS:** money and to **keep his web (and dirt-caches) intact**; survival via mutually-assured-destruction deals.
- **Attitude to crew:** cash-up-front services or a cultivable Asset. He can **get an Advisory on the Harrier cancelled** (~Cr25,000, a few weeks); **Warnings/arrest warrants** cost upward of Cr100,000 and aren't instant.
- **GM-Only:** killing him is famously fatal — allies are paid to avenge him; only "low-end idiots" try.

### Andrea Zumczizcy (starship-spares dealer)
- **Type:** Individual/Asset (could grow into a Faction). Runs a **legit ship-spares business** across several worlds (Class C/D ports).
- **Stat line (reconstruct/verify):** INT 9 EDU 9 SOC 8 · skills *Broker 3, Admin 2, Mechanic 1, Streetwise 2, Deception 1.*
- **Personality:** scrupulously, *visibly* clean — even hands dubious parts to the authorities at a loss to buy respectability.
- **WANTS:** to **expand into luxury & military starship markets**; she'll deal on anything that furthers that.
- **Attitude to crew:** can source spares for almost any ship given months' notice; passes on info from "the illegal side… that she has no part in."
- **GM-Only:** she **secretly runs an off-the-books salvage operation** dealing in **high-end engines, military-grade weapons/electronics and the occasional stolen starship** — and an associated **repossession/steal-to-order crew in the Dustbelt** that can snatch a specific vessel for the right price.

### Faction-patrons (Size/Type → see factions.md for the courting economy)
- **AYX Shipping Inc.** (Faction Size 6, Spacer; interaction MCr2.16) — small honest merchant line trusted by several Aslan clans; runs goods into the Hierate. WANTS: profit & preferential treatment. **Will quietly favour a reformed Drinax** (sail a blockade, hike prices, lend its rock-solid name to sway other worlds) — but **never does anything illegal**; would only act against rivals if untraceable.
- **LibArchy** (Faction Size 6, Social/Political; Cr216,000) — sprawling, decentralised anti-government movement. WANTS: no strong states. Useful early for **diversions, smuggling blind-eyes and "heroic outlaw" sympathy**; **becomes an enemy of a strong centralised Drinax** later.
- **The Bastion Movement** (Faction Size 5, Military; Cr375,000) — amateurish but fervent **anti-Aslan militia** across several Dustbelt worlds. WANTS: to "kick the *ihatei* out of the Dustbelt"; prestige & weapons. **An ally of Drinax waiting to happen** if professionalised — currently a noisy distraction and a market for serial-number-free guns; touchy about any pro-Aslan or unrelated tasking.
- **RCC-03** (Faction Size 4, Installation; Cr384,000) — struggling independent **mining outpost** on a Dustbelt moon, run by honest-but-desperate people. WANTS: a steady supply contract to break its middleman trap. **Perfect "don't-ask-don't-tell" pirate transit base/hideout** — a naval raid finds only a genuine, sympathetic mining settlement.
