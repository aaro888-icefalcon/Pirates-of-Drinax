## Combat round & action economy (Core p.73-75)
- Round = ~6 seconds. Map scale = 1.5 m per square/hex. Most fights start at Medium range.
- Per round each combatant gets: **1 Significant Action + 1 Minor Action**, OR **3 Minor Actions** (forgo Significant), plus unlimited **Reactions** and **Free Actions** (referee may cap).
- Act in Initiative order, highest first; ties broken by higher DEX, then simultaneous. A character takes both actions together on their turn. May freely delay.
- **Significant** (full attention): Attack; first aid; bypass security; use psionics; man a vehicle station; call ortillery.
- **Minor**: Aim; change stance (stand/crouch/prone); draw or reload (1 Minor each unless noted); move up to Movement (6 m for humans); spot/identify; pick up an item.
- **Free**: shout a warning, push a button.
- **Extended** (>1 round): roll a timeframe (Core p.62). If damaged mid-task, immediately re-roll that skill with the damage as a negative DM; fail = round wasted; fail by Effect -6 = task ruined.

## Initiative, surprise, Tactics, Leadership (Core p.73-74)
- Initiative = Effect of a **DEX or INT check** at combat start (pick the better characteristic). Same Initiative used every round.
- Referee may roll one Initiative for all NPCs on a side, using the highest DEX/INT.
- **Ambush/surprise (first round only):** aware side **DM+6** to Initiative; unaware side **DM-6**.
- **Tactics:** unless surprised, one character per side may make a **Tactics check** at combat start; its Effect is added to the Initiative of **everyone on that side**. (Military speciality for ground troops.)
- **Leadership** (Significant; INT/EDU/SOC): Effect = number of **DM+1** bonuses to distribute to allies' checks (incl. attacks). Must be able to communicate; no single check benefits from more than one +1. Negative Effect = the enemy hands out that many **DM-1** penalties to your side instead.

## Attack rolls (Core p.74, 76)
Target number is the standard **8+**.
- **Melee:** `2D + Melee (speciality) + STR or DEX DM` (use the better of STR/DEX).
- **Ranged (guns):** `2D + Gun Combat (speciality) + DEX DM`.
- Thrown weapons/grenades use **Athletics (dexterity)**; rocket/missile launchers use **Heavy Weapons (portable)** — substitute that skill for Gun Combat.
- Success (8+) deals damage; the **Effect is added to the damage roll**.

### Common attack modifiers (Core p.74)
| Bonus | DM | Penalty | DM |
|---|---|---|---|
| Aiming | +1 per Aim action (max +6) | Fast-moving target | -1 per full 10 m of relative movement |
| Laser sight | +1 (only while Aiming) | Long range | -2 |
| Short range | +1 | Extreme range | -4 |
| | | Target in cover | -2 |
| | | Prone target | -1 |

## Range bands & weapon range (Core p.76, p.83)
Each weapon lists a **Range** (m). Bands derived from it:
- **Short** = within ¼ Range → **DM+1**.
- Normal = up to Range → DM 0.
- **Long** = >Range to 2× Range → **DM-2**.
- **Extreme** = >2× Range to 4× Range → **DM-4**.
- Any shot **beyond 100 m is automatically Extreme** unless the weapon has **Scope** (then aim first to use true bands). No-stress environment: referee may raise the cap to 300 m.

Encounter range-band table (for narrative/sensor distance, Core p.83):
| Band | Distance |
|---|---|
| Close | ≤5 m |
| Short | 5-10 m |
| Medium | 11-50 m |
| Long | 51-250 m |
| Very Long | 251-500 m |
| Distant | 501 m-5 km |
| Very Distant | >5 km |

## Melee & close combat (Core p.76-78)
- **Close combat** = within 2 m. Melee attacks allowed; you and your foe may only attack those you're locked with.
- Only **one-handed ranged weapons** (pistols) may fire in close combat — and a pistol **can be parried** (knocked aside). Rifles etc. work only as **clubs**.
- A melee hit adds **STR DM** to damage (on top of Effect).
- If a combatant moves while locked in close combat, the enemy gets an immediate free attack at **DM+2**.
- **Grapple:** opposed Melee (unarmed), each using STR or DEX DM. Winner picks one: force prone; disarm (take weapon on Effect 6+); throw 1D m for 1D damage (ends grapple); deal `2 + Effect` damage ignoring armour; attack with pistol/small blade; drag 3 m; escape (move away); or hold. While grappling you may take no action but further opposed unarmed checks.
- **Two weapons** (one-handed each, referee's option): attack with both in a round, but no aiming and **DM-2 to both** attacks (no penalty if only one is used).

## Ranged traits & special fire (Core p.79)
- **Auto X:** three fire modes (aiming/Scope cannot combine with Auto):
  - *Single:* normal attack.
  - *Burst:* +Auto to damage; uses Auto rounds.
  - *Full Auto:* make Auto separate attacks (targets within 6 m of each other); uses 3× Auto rounds.
- **AP X:** ignores X points of Protection (vs spacecraft-scale armour only if the weapon is also spacecraft-scale).
- **Blast X:** hits everything within X m; **Dodge not allowed**, but targets may dive for cover; cover between target and blast centre applies.
- **Destructive (DD in damage):** multiply total damage rolled **×10**. Can blast apart small vehicles.
- **Radiation:** target takes **2D×20 rads** (×3 = 2D×60 for spacecraft-scale); if also Destructive, everyone within 10 m takes 2D×20 rads.
- **Stun:** damage to **END only** (after Protection). END→0 = incapacitated for (excess) rounds; fully healed by **1 hour rest**.
- **Smart:** DM = TL difference attacker-weapon vs target (armour/vehicle/device), min +1, max +6.
- **Scope:** ignores the 100 m→Extreme rule if you aim first.
- **Bulky:** needs STR 9+ or take DM = (STR DM − +1). **Very Bulky:** needs STR 12+ or DM = (STR DM − +2).
- **Zero-G:** no recoil; usable in low/zero-G without the Athletics check.

## Reactions (Core p.75-76)
Unlimited per round, **but each Reaction gives DM-1 to your next set of actions** (cumulative). Each incoming attack must be reacted to separately.
- **Dodge** (melee or ranged): attacker suffers DM = your **DEX DM or Athletics (dexterity)**, whichever is higher.
- **Dive for cover** (ranged only): DM-2 to every attacker this round AND may gain bonus Protection from cover within 1.5 m (if none, only DM-1 as a prone target). **You forfeit your next actions entirely.**
- **Parry** (close combat, vs melee): attacker suffers DM = your **Melee skill**. Rapier gives +1 to parry; Shield raises effective Melee +1 to parry (Melee 1 if untrained).

## Cover & hiding (Core p.77)
- Using cover: **DM-2** to all ranged attacks against you.
- **Hiding** (fully out of sight, no attacks): gain cover benefit; if a foe still knows you're there, you also gain **bonus Protection** by cover type. Two cover types don't stack — use the best.

| Cover | Bonus Protection |
|---|---|
| Vegetation | +2 |
| Tree trunk | +6 |
| Stone wall | +8 |
| Civilian vehicle | +10 |
| Armoured vehicle | +15 |
| Fortifications | +20 |

## Damage & characteristics (Core p.77)
1. On a hit, roll the weapon's damage dice **+ the attack's Effect**.
2. Subtract **Armour Protection** (see below).
3. Apply remaining damage to **END** first.
4. Excess past 0 END goes to **STR or DEX** (target's choice).
5. If **either STR or DEX hits 0 → unconscious**; further damage goes to the last physical characteristic.
6. **All three (STR, DEX, END) at 0 → dead.**
- Recompute DMs from reduced characteristics and use the impaired DMs until healed.

## Armour & AP (Core p.76-77)
- Armour reduces damage by its **Protection**. An attack with **Effect 6+ always deals at least 1 damage** regardless of Protection or the roll.
- **AP X** weapons ignore X points of Protection.
- Armour does **not** protect against falling damage (Core p.80).

## Severe wounds & hit locations — OPTIONAL (Companion 2024 p.53-55)
> The Core personal system has **no** crit table; wound severity is read off the damage relative to starting END. Use these when you want lasting injury (boarding actions, named foes).

Wound thresholds (per single instance, vs starting END):
- **Minor** (< ½ END): no extra effect.
- **Major** (½ END to full END): impairs the hit body part; heal with **Routine (6+) Medic** + D3 days recuperation.
- **Severe** (> END): **knocked unconscious**, lasting effects; heal with **Average (8+) Medic** + 1D days, then Major-wound effects for another D3 days.
- **> 2× END:** likely dead. Extended (cinematic) effects if wanted:
  - **Crippling (≥2× END):** location out of action, permanent; random STR/END/DEX -D3.
  - **Critical (≥3× END):** location out of action; STR/END/DEX each -D3+1.
  - **Mortal (≥4× END):** usually lethal; shears off limb / decapitates.
  - **Devastating (≥5× END):** location destroyed/vaporised.

Hit location: roll **1D** — 1 Head, 2 Arms, 3-5 Torso, 6 Legs. Default = best armour on that location applies. **Called shot** to a location: **DM-2** (or **DM-6** with rapid fire); failure = clean miss. Use to bypass partial armour/cover.

| Location | Major Wound | Severe Wound |
|---|---|---|
| Head | DM-1 all checks | DM-2 all checks |
| Arms | DM-1 checks using arms | DM-1 checks using arms |
| Torso | DM-1 physical, speed -1 m | DM-2 physical; can only crawl unless supported |
| Legs | speed -2 m, DM-1 move checks | leg disabled; only hobble with support, no move actions |

Boarding note: melee/blades are preferred when **capturing** a ship — far less likely than guns to breach the hull or wreck systems, but good at opening a self-sealing vacc suit (Core p.124).

## Unconsciousness & death (Core p.83)
- **Unconscious:** make an **END check each minute**; success = wake. Each failure adds **cumulative DM+1** to the next attempt (after a 1-minute wait).
- **Death:** all three physical characteristics at 0 (see Damage). No "death save" — it is final.

## Healing (Core p.82-83)
Two forms: medical treatment and natural. Failed Medic checks can **harm** the patient.
1. **First Aid** (Medic, EDU): restores points = **Effect** (min 1), split among damaged physicals as desired. Needs ≥ basic medical gear (medikit), must start **within 1 minute** of injury, usable **once** per injury.
2. **Surgery** (Medic, EDU; needs hospital/sickbay): used when **3 physicals still damaged** after first aid. Restores like first aid, but a **failed** check costs the patient **3 + Effect** more points.
3. **Medical care** (hospital/sickbay + full bed rest): when **≤2 physicals damaged** (or one restored by surgery). Restores **3 + patient's END DM + doctor's Medic** points/day, split evenly. (High-tech hospitals use **Slow Drug** to accelerate.)
4. **Natural healing:** **1D + END DM** points/day of full rest. If surgery was needed, only **END DM**/day (so a negative END DM means no natural healing — and possible worsening).
- **Augment penalty:** medical care/surgery suffers DM = TL gap between facility and the patient's highest relevant implant.
- **Mental characteristics** (INT/EDU, not PSI) heal **1 point each/day**.

## Hazards (Core p.80-82; vacuum/decompression detail Companion 2024 p.66-67)
- **Falling:** 1D per 2 m (1-G world). High/low-G: ±1D per 4 m. Successful Athletics check reduces distance-for-damage by its Effect (m). **Armour does not protect.**
- **Vacuum (hard):** cumulative **1D/round** (1D round 1, 2D round 2, 3D round 3…). Damage starts with END.
- **Suffocation:** no life support = **1D/minute**; utterly airless (smothered/strangled/spaced) = **1D/round**. A sealed structure with power supports ~1 person/stateroom for 1 month comfortably (6 months max; staterooms ×5,000 person-hours); no power → ~2 weeks.
- **Decompression** (Companion, optional detail):
  - *Explosive* (sudden major hole/suit shred): occupants flung — **Average (8+) Vacc Suit or Athletics (dex)** to grab hold or be propelled at the breach; striking something = **2D** but a second grab attempt; a final Vacc Suit/Athletics check or go out the hole = **+3D**. Sequence: **Round 0 explosive effects → Round 1 partial vacuum → Round 2+ hard vacuum.**
  - *Rapid* (minor breach): Round 0 noticeable, no harm; referee rolls D3 (rounds of normal air), then D3 (minimal pressure), then D3 (partial vacuum), then hard vacuum.
  - **Minimal pressure:** tolerable for END minutes if you have air; after that END -1/min, then unconscious at 0, then STR/DEX alternating to death.
  - **Partial vacuum:** **1D/round** (then unconscious/death as above).
- **Temperature (starts with END):**
| Heat | Effect | Cold | Effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| 50° (hot desert) | 1D/hour | -25° (Arctic) | 1D/hour |
| 200° (~Mercury) | 1D/round | -50° (~Mars) | 2D/hour |
| 500° (~Venus) | 2D/round | -200° (~Pluto) | 1D/round |
| Burning torch | 1D/round | Industrial freezer | 1D/round |
| Welding torch | 2D/round | Liquid nitrogen | 2D/round |
| Inferno | 3D/round | | |
- **Radiation** (rads accumulate permanently; only **anti-rad drugs** remove accumulated rads). Spacecraft hull cuts exposure by **500**; vacc suits also reduce. Exposure rates: minor reactor leak 2D/hour; serious leak 2D/20 min; minor solar flare 1D×100/hour; major flare 3D×100/hour; Radiation weapon 2D×20 once.

| Dose (immediate) | Immediate effect | Cumulative total | Permanent effect |
|---|---|---|---|
| ≤50 | none | ≤50 | none |
| 51-150 | 1D dmg, Nausea (-1 all checks until treated) | 51-150 | none |
| 151-300 | 2D dmg | 151-300 | -1 END |
| 301-500 | 4D dmg, hair loss | 301-500 | -2 END |
| 501-800 | 6D dmg, sterile | 501-800 | -3 END |
| 801+ | 8D dmg, internal bleeding | 801+ | -4 END |

- **Gravity** (acclimatise in 1D weeks; with Athletics it's 1D days): **High-G** (≥1.4 G) **DM-1 all checks** (Athletics *strength* speeds it); **Low-G** (≤0.7 G) **DM-1 physical checks** (Athletics *dexterity* speeds it); **Zero-G** = Low-G effects + firing a recoil weapon (no Zero-G trait) needs **Average (8+) Athletics (dex)** or auto-miss and spin out (another 8+ Athletics dex to recover).
- **Fatigue:** triggered by staying awake > END+18 hours, heavy labour > END hours, or > END consecutive melee attacks in one fight. Effect: **DM-2 to all checks** until rested (may collapse if pushed further).
- **Weather:** high wind/torrential rain **DM-1** to checks in it.

## Boarding actions (abstract resolution) (Core p.175)
Once ships are within ~1 km (Adjacent), marines may launch a boarding action in the Actions Step; it takes **2D rounds** to resolve. Both sides roll **2D + modifiers**; defender subtracts their total from attacker's; compare result.

Modifiers: Superior Armour +1; Superior Weaponry +1; Superior Skills/Tactics +2; Superior Numbers +1; Vastly Superior Numbers +3; defender has **no marines on duty -2**.

| Net result | Outcome |
|---|---|
| ≤ -7 | Attackers crushed; if docked, defenders may counter-board at **DM+4**. |
| -4 to -6 | Attackers defeated; must retreat or be killed/captured. |
| -1 to -3 | Fighting continues; re-resolve in 1D rounds, defender **DM+2**; ship loses **2D Hull**. |
| 0 | Fighting continues; re-resolve in 1D rounds. |
| 1 to 3 | Fighting continues; re-resolve in 1D rounds, attacker **DM+2**; ship loses **2D Hull**. |
| 4 to 6 | Success; ship takes **1D damage (ignores armour)**; attackers control it after 2D rounds of pacification. |
| 7+ | Attackers storm and take control immediately. |

Any "fighting continues" result may instead be **played out at Ground scale** — the boarders have a beachhead and the crew can pick targets and tactics inside the ship. **Docking** an unresisting ship = Routine (6+) Pilot (1D min); airlocks cycle in 10 s, hold 3 vacc-suited people, are bridge-locked (Very Difficult 12+ Electronics/computers to override), and usually have lockers with vacc suits, rescue bubbles, and cutlasses nearby (Core p.152).
