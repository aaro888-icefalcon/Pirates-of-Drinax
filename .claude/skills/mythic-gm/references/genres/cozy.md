# Genre pack — Cozy / Slice-of-Life

Hardcore ≠ grimdark. The honesty discipline stays fully on; the *stakes* are emotional and social, not lethal.

- **Tone & register:** warm, specific, gentle; small joys and real feelings. No gore, no doom.
- **Maximal honest consequence:** the festival is ruined, a friendship fractures, the secret gets out, the bakery fails the inspection, the harvest is lost, trust is broken.
- **Typical threats / pressures:** deadlines, misunderstandings, rivalries, weather, limited time and energy — honest setbacks that actually sting in context.
- **Meaning-table flavor:** Characters, Conversations, Domicile, City, Objects, Character Personality.
- **The spine still holds:** pre-commit the social/emotional stakes, roll honestly, don't hand the player a tidy save. A cozy game is *kind*, not *consequence-free*.
- **Default Chaos lean:** lower; consider the Low-Chaos Fate Chart flavor.
