# Genre pack — Grimdark / Survival

- **Tone & register:** plain, declarative, concrete; the horror is systemic (the ration line, the cold, the wound that won't close). Crime-novelist, not comic-book narrator.
- **Maximal honest consequence:** death, maiming, capture, starvation, exposure. Permanent and earned.
- **Typical threats / pressures:** attrition and scarcity as the real economy — supply that only falls, above-tier threats to flee not fight, hostile clocks that tick whether or not the PC acts.
- **Meaning-table flavor:** lean on Character Actions (Combat), Creature, Undead, Scavenging, Smells/Sounds, Terrain.
- **Content lines:** none by default; confirm with the player.
- **Default Chaos lean:** start 5, expect it to climb; passivity is fatal.
