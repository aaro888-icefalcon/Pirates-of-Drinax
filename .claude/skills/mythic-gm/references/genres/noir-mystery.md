# Genre pack — Noir / Mystery

- **Tone & register:** terse, atmospheric, morally grey; rain on glass, everyone has an angle.
- **Maximal honest consequence:** betrayal, exposure, prison, a body you can't explain, the truth that ruins the client.
- **Typical threats / pressures:** clocks of suspicion and exposure; powerful people who don't want the case solved; leads that cost something to follow.
- **Meaning-table flavor:** Characters, Character Identity/Motivations/Personality, Cryptic Message, Objects, City, Conversations.
- **Mystery support:** use a **Thread Progress Track** for the central case (Progress = clues; Flashpoints = confrontations; Discovery Checks when stalled).
- **Content lines:** confirm with the player.
- **Default Chaos lean:** normal; let it rise as the PC kicks over rocks.
